About Me

Jonathan Marcantel graduated with honors from the University of South Florida, where he studied Business Analytics and Information Systems. As a student, he was a member of the Business Analytics and Information Systems Club, the Bulls Against Bullying Club, and the Institute of Industrial and Systems Engineers Chapter at USF. He is a hard worker who loves to manage teams.

In 2014, he worked on the game Beneath as a writer and narrative designer. In 2017, he interned as a business intern at Celebration Pediatrics. He has also managed project teams in creating databases for classes at the university. In 2021, he worked on the Developer Operations Policy Team at Meta (via Accenture Flex). In this role, he tested third-party applications and reported findings to third-party application developers. In 2022, he worked for Verisk as a Business Analyst under the Enterprise Data & Shared Service Team. He was in charge of managing and maintaining the organization of their data science data warehouse.

He is currently working as a Data Signal Analyst at Google in Austin, Texas. Jonathan has had a passion for technology and business ever since he attended a business and innovation conference at Stanford University in the summer of 2014. This experience inspired him to change the world and innovate with his talents.
